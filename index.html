Vibe Coding Guidebook

Welcome to the era of pure creation. Focus on the vision, design, and vibes; let AI handle the heavy lifting.

🌊

What is Vibe Coding?

Vibe coding is a development philosophy where the programmer steps into the role of a creative director. Using state-of-the-art AI code generators, you dictate layout ideas, desired mechanics, and design languages, guiding the model through iterative prompting rather than manual keyboard typing.

🚀

How to Get Started

To begin vibe coding, choose a modern AI assistant. Start by explaining your project goals in plain language, design rules, and visual style. Feed files directly to the model, run its suggestions in your browser, and prompt it to polish, fix bugs, or add details until you are completely satisfied.

🎉

Vibe Coding Parties

Every month, we host themed hackathons where creators build biological, ecological, or aesthetic projects in just a few hours. No prior programming background is required! It's a space for scientists, artists, and creators to bring concepts to life in a collaborative, energetic environment.

🙌

How to Get Involved

Ready to join? You can attend our monthly physical meetups, hop into our community channels, or build on your own using the themes listed in this gallery. Simply click the "Share Project" button at the top to contribute your vibe-coded masterpiece!
